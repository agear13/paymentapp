# INTEGRATED EVENT PRODUCTION PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

**Date:** 3 March 2027

**Producer:** **Stagecraft Global Pty Ltd** (ABN 74 889 990 221) ("Production Partner")  
**Rights Owner:** **Harbour Lights Pty Ltd** (ABN 85 990 001 332) ("Rights Owner")

## 1. Project

Design, build, and operate production for **Harbour Lights New Year's Eve 2027** including main stage, broadcast compound, and river barges.

## 2. Commercial model

**Production Budget Cap:** AUD $4.2 million (exclusive of GST).

Funding:

- Rights Owner funds **65%** upfront per approved cash flow;  
- Production Partner contributes **35%** as deferred production services credit.

## 3. Revenue participation

After recovery of:

1. direct third-party costs;  
2. Production Partner deferred credit with **8% priority return**;  
3. Rights Owner advance recovery;

remaining **Net Event Profit** splits **55% Rights Owner / 45% Production Partner**.

**Net Event Profit** includes sponsorship, broadcast rights, and on-site F&B participation attributable to production-managed areas per Schedule F.

## 4. Production Partner scope

- technical direction and crew hiring;  
- safety case and Work Health and Safety coordination;  
- vendor RFP management;  
- show caller and artist liaison;  
- strike and site remediation.

## 5. Rights Owner scope

- council and maritime permits;  
- talent contracts;  
- sponsor sales;  
- insurance certificates naming Production Partner as additional insured.

## 6. Cash flow

Fortnightly drawdowns against approved budget. Variations over AUD $75,000 require joint steering committee approval within **72 hours**.

## 7. Warranties

Production Partner warrants structural certifications for stage and barges. Rights Owner warrants permit validity.

## 8. Delay and force majeure

If event delayed by government order, parties negotiate extension of deferred credit recovery timeline.

## 9. Termination

If either party fails steering committee milestones twice consecutively, the other may assume control by paying outstanding verified costs plus **15%** facilitation fee.

## 10. Disputes

Mediation in Sydney before arbitration under ACICA rules.

## 11. Governing law

New South Wales.

**Signed**
