# PROMOTER, DJ AND VENUE SETTLEMENT AGREEMENT

**Date:** 9 June 2026

**Parties:**

- **Basement Club Pty Ltd** (ABN 12 998 776 554) ("Venue");  
- **Voltage Promotions Pty Ltd** (ABN 23 887 665 443) ("Promoter");  
- **DJ Cassidy Tran** (ABN 34 776 554 332) ("Artist").

## 1. Event

The parties will collaborate on **"Voltage Fridays"** at Basement Club on the first Friday of each month from July to September 2026.

## 2. Revenue pool

**Adjusted Gross Receipts (AGR)** means door sales, table deposits, and walk-in bar spend attributable to the event, less GST, refunds, and merchant fees.

## 3. Revenue allocation

After deduction of a **AUD $1,200 venue production reserve** per night:

| Beneficiary | Percentage of remaining AGR |
|-------------|---------------------------|
| Voltage Promotions Pty Ltd | 45% |
| DJ Cassidy Tran | 25% |
| Basement Club Pty Ltd | 30% |

## 4. Artist guarantee

The Venue and Promoter jointly guarantee the Artist a minimum **AUD $2,000** per night. If the Artist's 25% share plus any appearance fee allocation falls below the guarantee, the Promoter tops up **60%** and the Venue **40%** of the shortfall.

## 5. Settlement

Promoter prepares a settlement sheet within **48 hours** of each event. All parties receive payment within **5 business days**.

## 6. Obligations

**Promoter:** marketing, guest list management, settlement reporting.  
**Artist:** 90-minute DJ set, promotional posts (2) within 7 days before each event.  
**Venue:** staffing, liquor licence, sound system maintenance.

## 7. Termination

Material breach may be terminated on **14 days' notice** if not remedied. If the Artist cancels without medical certificate, the Artist forfeits the guarantee for that night.

## 8. Law

Victoria, Australia.

**Signed by all parties**
