# BEACH CLUB EVENT COLLABORATION AGREEMENT

**Date:** 18 August 2026

**Between:**

- **Azure Beach Club Pty Ltd** (ABN 67 889 990 221) ("Beach Club");  
- **Sunset Sessions Pty Ltd** (ABN 78 990 001 332) ("Event Producer").

## 1. Event series

Event Producer curates **"Sunset Sundays"** poolside events at Azure Beach Club each Sunday in October 2026.

## 2. Commercial terms

Event Producer pays a **facility access fee of AUD $4,000 per Sunday**. Beach Club retains **100% of F&B sales** processed through its POS. Merchandise sold by Event Producer belongs to Event Producer.

## 3. Event Producer obligations

- obtain permits for amplified music;  
- provide lifeguard coordination for pool activations;  
- supply DJ talent and signage;  
- carry event public liability insurance of AUD $20 million naming Beach Club as additional insured.

## 4. Beach Club obligations

- provide 4 cabanas and pool deck access;  
- maintain liquor licence and RSA staff;  
- provide power and basic lighting.

## 5. Payment timing

Facility fee invoiced **7 days before** each Sunday; payable within **3 business days** of invoice.

## 6. Weather cancellation

If wind exceeds 45 km/h, parties postpone by mutual agreement without penalty.

## 7. Termination

Either party may terminate the series with **21 days' notice** after the first two events.

## 8. Law

Western Australia.
