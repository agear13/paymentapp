# EVENT SECURITY SERVICES AGREEMENT

**Date:** 1 May 2026

**Parties:**

1. **Westside Venues Pty Ltd** (ABN 66 777 888 999) ("Client");  
2. **Guardian Event Security Pty Ltd** (ABN 77 888 999 000) ("Security Provider").

## 1. Services

Security Provider will supply **12 licensed security officers** for a concert at Westside Arena on **17 May 2026** from 4:00 pm to 1:00 am.

## 2. Fees

Fixed fee: **AUD $6,720 inclusive of GST**, invoiced after the event and payable within **7 days**.

## 3. Security Provider obligations

- all personnel hold current crowd control licences;  
- supervisor on site from door open to close;  
- incident log submitted within 24 hours.

## 4. Client obligations

Client provides briefing on evacuation routes and medical plan.

## 5. Termination

Client may reduce headcount by up to 2 officers with 72 hours' notice; fees adjusted pro-rata.

## 6. Liability

Security Provider's liability capped at fees paid under this agreement.

**Signed**
