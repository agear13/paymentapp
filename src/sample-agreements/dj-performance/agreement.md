# DJ PERFORMANCE AND BAR PARTICIPATION AGREEMENT

**Date:** 11 July 2026

**Parties:**

- **The Dock Venue Pty Ltd** (ABN 45 667 889 001) ("Venue");  
- **Nina Sol Entertainment** (ABN 56 778 990 112) ("DJ").

## 1. Performance

DJ will perform on **26 July 2026** from 10:00 pm to 2:00 am at The Dock Venue, Fremantle WA.

## 2. Performance fee and bar share

(a) Fixed performance fee: **AUD $3,500 plus GST**.  
(b) In addition, DJ receives **15% of Net Bar Sales** recorded between 9:00 pm and 3:00 am on the performance night.

**Net Bar Sales** means bar revenue less staff tabs and voids.

## 3. Payment

Performance fee payable **7 days before** the event. Bar share calculated from POS report and paid within **10 business days** after the event.

## 4. DJ obligations

- provide own headphones and USB backups;  
- refrain from playing unlicensed bootlegs;  
- participate in one promotional interview arranged by Venue.

## 5. Venue obligations

Provide Pioneer CDJ-3000 setup, green room, and standard hospitality rider.

## 6. Cancellation

If Venue cancels, DJ receives full fee. If DJ cancels less than 14 days out, DJ refunds 50% of any deposit received.

## 7. Termination for breach

Immediate termination if either party breaches liquor or safety laws.
