## 1. Hire of premises

The Venue agrees to hire the Grand Hall main ballroom to the Hirer for a private product launch on **19 March 2026** from 2:00 pm to 10:00 pm.

## 2. Hire fee

The Hirer shall pay a fixed hire fee of **AUD $8,500 (exclusive of GST)**.

**Payment schedule:**

- 50% deposit due on signing;  
- balance due **14 days before** the event date.

## 3. Hirer obligations

The Hirer must:

1. comply with capacity limits (maximum 350 guests);  
2. engage licensed security if attendance exceeds 200 guests;  
3. leave the premises in clean condition by 10:30 pm;  
4. indemnify the Venue against damage caused by Hirer's guests.

## 4. Cancellation

If the Hirer cancels less than 14 days before the event, the deposit is non-refundable.

## 5. Termination

The Venue may terminate immediately if the Hirer breaches liquor licensing conditions.
