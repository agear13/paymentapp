# FESTIVAL CO-PRODUCTION AND PROFIT PARTICIPATION AGREEMENT

**Date:** 21 April 2027

**Co-Producer A:** **Wide Sky Festivals Pty Ltd** (ABN 96 112 233 445)  
**Co-Producer B:** **Southern Trails Pty Ltd** (ABN 97 223 344 556)

## 1. Festival

The parties co-produce **Wide Sky Folk & Roots 2027**, a three-day camping festival in Victoria (expected attendance 12,000).

## 2. Governance

Joint steering committee with equal votes. Deadlock referred to independent festival producer within **7 days**.

## 3. Capital calls

Each party contributes **50%** of approved funding rounds. Initial capital call **AUD $600,000** within **10 business days** of signing.

## 4. Revenue

Festival revenue includes tickets, camping, merch, bar profit share (via venue services contract), and sponsorship.

## 5. Cost allocation

Direct costs booked to festival cost centre. Shared overhead (office, accounting) split 50/50.

## 6. Profit distribution

**Distributable Cash** paid quarterly:

1. repay member loans with **5% simple interest**;  
2. recoup approved pre-production advances;  
3. balance distributed **50% / 50%**.

## 7. Artist and vendor contracting

Contracts over AUD $40,000 require both producers' signatures. Preferred suppliers listed in Schedule G.

## 8. Insurance

Joint names on event cancellation and public liability policies minimum AUD $30 million.

## 9. Regulatory

Co-Producer A leads council liaison; Co-Producer B leads liquor licensing.

## 10. Exit

If one party wishes to exit before ticket on-sale, the other may buy their interest at independent valuation less **10%** discount, or wind down by mutual consent.

## 11. Termination for cause

Fraud, unapproved material cost overrun, or failure to fund capital call within **5 business days** triggers buy-out right.

## 12. Law

Victoria.

**Signed**
