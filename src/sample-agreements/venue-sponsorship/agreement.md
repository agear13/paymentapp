# VENUE SPONSORSHIP AND BAR REVENUE PARTICIPATION DEED

**Date:** 22 April 2026

**Between:**

- **Skyline Rooftop Bar Pty Ltd** (ABN 91 223 445 667) ("Venue"); and  
- **Pacific Spirits Australia Pty Ltd** (ABN 82 334 556 778) ("Sponsor"), distributor of **Pacific Tonic** premium mixers.

## 1. Sponsorship grant

Sponsor provides:

- AUD $15,000 cash sponsorship for summer branding;  
- branded fridge units;  
- promotional staff for 6 activation nights.

## 2. Exclusive pourage

Pacific Tonic is the exclusive premium mixer in the main bar for **90 days** from 1 May 2026.

## 3. Revenue participation

In addition to the cash sponsorship, Sponsor receives **8% of Net Premium Mixer Sales** collected at the Venue during the exclusivity period.

**Net Premium Mixer Sales** excludes staff comps and happy-hour discounted pours.

## 4. Reporting and payment

Venue provides monthly sales reports by the **5th business day** of the following month. Revenue share payments due **14 days** after each report.

## 5. Sponsor obligations

Sponsor maintains brand guidelines, supplies POS kits, and indemnifies Venue for product liability claims arising from defective stock supplied by Sponsor.

## 6. Venue obligations

Venue displays branding as per schedule, trains bar staff on pour recipes, and does not promote competing premium mixers in the main bar.

## 7. Termination

Either party may terminate if sales reports are late by more than 15 days on two occasions. Sponsor must remove branding within 10 days of termination.

## 8. Governing law

New South Wales.

**Executed as a deed**
