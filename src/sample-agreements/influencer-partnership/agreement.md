# INFLUENCER MARKETING AND ATTRIBUTED REVENUE SHARE AGREEMENT

**Date:** 19 February 2027

**Brand:** **GlowFest Pty Ltd** (ABN 41 556 778 990) ("Festival Owner")  
**Influencer:** **Chloe Nguyen** (ABN 52 667 889 001) ("Creator")  
**Agency:** **Social Bridge Management Pty Ltd** (ABN 63 778 990 112) ("Manager")

## 1. Campaign

Creator promotes GlowFest 2027 ticket sales via Instagram, TikTok, and newsletter from 1 March to 30 April 2027.

## 2. Compensation structure

(a) **Base fee:** AUD $8,500 inclusive of GST for eight deliverables.  
(b) **Attribution commission:** **6% of Net Ticket Revenue** from sales using Creator's code **GLOWCHLOE**.  
(c) **Tier bonus:** additional **2%** if attributed sales exceed AUD $250,000 in the campaign window.

**Net Ticket Revenue** excludes refunds and complimentary staff tickets.

## 3. Content obligations

Creator delivers:

- 4x Instagram feed posts;  
- 2x TikTok videos (minimum 30 seconds);  
- 2x story sets (minimum 6 frames each);  
- 1x newsletter feature (subject to list owner approval).

All captions must include ACCC-compliant disclosure (#ad) and approved ticket link.

## 4. Manager obligations

Manager invoices base fee, collects commission statements, and ensures Creator compliance with Platform Community Guidelines.

## 5. Festival Owner obligations

Provide creative assets within **5 business days** of request; supply real-time attribution dashboard; pay commission **30 days** after month-end.

## 6. Exclusivity

Creator will not promote competing music festivals in Queensland within **21 days** before and after campaign flight.

## 7. Morals clause

Festival Owner may suspend campaign if Creator is subject to public conduct controversy likely to harm brand value, pending good-faith discussion.

## 8. IP licence

Creator grants Festival Owner a 12-month licence to repost approved assets with credit.

## 9. Termination

Either party terminates on **14 days' notice**; commissions still payable on sales prior to termination date.

## 10. Law

Queensland.

**Signed by Festival Owner, Creator, and Manager**
