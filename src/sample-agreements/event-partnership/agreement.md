# CO-PROMOTION PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

**Date:** 30 June 2026

**Party A:** **Luna Hospitality Group Pty Ltd** (ABN 81 223 445 667)  
**Party B:** **Rhythm Media Pty Ltd** (ABN 92 334 556 778)

## 1. Partnership purpose

The parties co-create and co-market **"Luna x Rhythm — Winter Sessions"**, a three-date live music series in Sydney.

## 2. Contribution matrix

| Party | Contribution |
|-------|--------------|
| Luna Hospitality Group | venue, bar operations, security |
| Rhythm Media | artist booking, paid media, content production |

## 3. Profit share

Net Profit (ticket revenue plus bar gross profit less direct costs) split **50% / 50%**.

## 4. Decision making

Operational decisions require mutual email approval. Either party may veto artist bookings above AUD $25,000 fee.

## 5. Banking

All event receipts flow to a joint operating account managed by Luna. Rhythm receives profit distributions within **10 business days** after each show reconciliation.

## 6. Branding

Joint branding guidelines attached as Schedule A. Social content approvals within **48 hours**.

## 7. Term

Covers three events in August 2026. Either party may withdraw from remaining dates on **30 days' notice**, forfeiting share of future profits but not prior settled amounts.

## 8. Governing law

New South Wales.

**Signed**
