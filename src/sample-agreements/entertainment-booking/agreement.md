**Between:**

- **Coastal Festivals Ltd** (ABN 22 334 455 667) ("Promoter");

The Artist shall perform a **60-minute headline set** at Coastal Fest on **28 December 2026** at Coffs Harbour Parklands.

## 2. Artist fee

Promoter shall pay an all-inclusive artist fee of **AUD $18,000 (GST inclusive)**.

Payment terms:

- 50% deposit on signing;  
- 50% balance **no later than 7 days before** the performance date.

## 3. Promoter obligations

The Promoter provides backline, hospitality rider, and domestic flights for two artists.

## 4. Cancellation

If Promoter cancels for reasons other than force majeure, the deposit is forfeited to the Artist.

## 5. Exclusivity

The Artist will not perform within 100 km of the festival within 14 days before or after the event.
