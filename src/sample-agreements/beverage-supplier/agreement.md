# EXCLUSIVE BEVERAGE SUPPLY AGREEMENT

**Date:** 14 October 2026

**Licensee:** **Metro Arena Pty Ltd** (ABN 53 667 889 990)  
**Supplier:** **Southern Draft Co Pty Ltd** (ABN 64 778 990 001)

## 1. Exclusive supply

Supplier is exclusive draught beer provider for public events at Metro Arena from 1 November 2026 to 31 March 2027.

## 2. Supply terms

Supplier provides kegs at agreed wholesale schedule **Schedule B**. Supplier rebates **AUD $2.80 per equivalent litre** sold above 40,000 litres in the term.

## 3. Supplier obligations

- delivery within 24 hours of order;  
- line cleaning every 14 days;  
- branded tap handles;  
- product recall cooperation.

## 4. Licensee obligations

- minimum annual throughput of **32,000 litres** or pay shortfall fee of AUD $0.90 per litre;  
- report monthly volumes by **3rd business day**;  
- store kegs at 4°C.

## 5. Payment

Wholesale invoices **14 days** from delivery. Rebates credited quarterly.

## 6. Termination

Licensee may terminate if two consecutive deliveries are more than 48 hours late. Supplier may terminate if throughput falls below 70% of minimum for two quarters.

## 7. Governing law

South Australia.
