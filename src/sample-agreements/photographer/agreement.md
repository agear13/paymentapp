# EVENT PHOTOGRAPHY AND CONTENT LICENCE AGREEMENT

**Date:** 5 May 2027

**Client:** **Kite Music Pty Ltd** (ABN 58 667 889 990) ("Promoter")  
**Photographer:** **Lena Ortiz Photography Pty Ltd** (ABN 69 778 990 001) ("Photographer")

## 1. Engagement

Photographer captures still images and limited b-roll at **Kite Music Weekender** on 19–20 June 2027.

## 2. Fees

(a) **Day rate:** AUD $2,200 per day (2 days).  
(b) **Rush delivery fee:** AUD $900 if edited gallery delivered within **24 hours** of each day.  
(c) **Licensing uplift:** Promoter pays **AUD $1,200** for 12-month global marketing licence for up to 50 images.

Total potential contract value: **AUD $6,500 plus GST**.

## 3. Deliverables

- minimum 400 RAW files per day;  
- 60 edited hero images within **72 hours**;  
- metadata keywording with artist names where identifiable;  
- secure cloud transfer via Client-approved platform.

## 4. Photographer obligations

- carry professional indemnity and equipment insurance;  
- wear provided media wristband;  
- no flash during headline DJ sets without stage manager approval;  
- deliver chain-of-title model releases where provided on site.

## 5. Promoter obligations

- provide media pit access and accreditation list;  
- credit Photographer on social posts where images used;  
- pay invoices within **14 days**.

## 6. IP and moral rights

Photographer retains copyright. Promoter receives licence per clause 2(c). Photographer consents to reasonable cropping for social formats.

## 7. Exclusivity

Photographer will not license competing festival images shot at same event to third parties for **90 days** without Promoter approval.

## 8. Cancellation

If festival day cancelled for weather, Photographer paid **50%** day rate plus documented travel costs capped at AUD $400.

## 9. Liability cap

Photographer liability limited to fees paid except for breach of confidentiality.

## 10. Governing law

New South Wales.
