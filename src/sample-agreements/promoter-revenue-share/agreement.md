# NIGHTLIFE PROMOTION AND DOOR RECEIPTS AGREEMENT

**Date:** 14 January 2026

**Between:**

1. **Harbour Events Pty Ltd** (ABN 81 234 567 890) of Level 4, 120 Crown Street, Sydney NSW 2010 ("Venue Operator"); and  
2. **Pulse Promotions Pty Ltd** (ABN 92 345 678 901) of PO Box 441, Surry Hills NSW 2010 ("Promoter").

## 1. Engagement

The Venue Operator appoints the Promoter as exclusive promoter for the event **"Harbour Saturdays — Summer Series"** on the following dates: 8 February, 15 February, and 22 February 2026 (each an "Event Night").

## 2. Promoter obligations

The Promoter shall:

(a) market each Event Night using its mailing list and social channels;  
(b) supply approved artwork within 5 business days of signing;  
(c) provide a door staff roster of no fewer than two ticket scanners;  
(d) remit the Venue Operator's share of Net Door Receipts within **3 business days** after each Event Night;  
(e) maintain public liability insurance of at least AUD $20 million.

## 3. Revenue share

**Net Door Receipts** means gross ticket and door sales collected at the venue on an Event Night, less card processing fees and refunds.

The parties agree to the following split of Net Door Receipts:

| Party | Share |
|-------|-------|
| Pulse Promotions Pty Ltd (Promoter) | **70%** |
| Harbour Events Pty Ltd (Venue Operator) | **30%** |

## 4. Payment timing

Settlement statements must be emailed to accounts@harbourevents.com.au by 5:00 pm on the second business day following each Event Night. Payment of the Venue Operator's share is due **3 business days** after the Event Night.

## 5. Term and termination

This agreement commences on the date above and ends on 23 February 2026. Either party may terminate on **7 days' written notice** if the other party breaches a material term and fails to remedy within 5 business days.

## 6. Governing law

New South Wales, Australia.
