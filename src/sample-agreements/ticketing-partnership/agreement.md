# TICKETING PLATFORM, PROMOTER AND VENUE TRIPARTITE AGREEMENT

**Date:** 12 January 2027

**Parties:**

1. **Arena One Pty Ltd** (ABN 10 111 222 333) ("Venue");  
2. **Frontline Live Pty Ltd** (ABN 20 222 333 444) ("Promoter");  
3. **TicketRiver Australia Pty Ltd** (ABN 30 333 444 555) ("Ticketing Platform").

## 1. Background

The parties will deliver a six-date arena tour **"Frontline Live Winter Tour 2027"** using TicketRiver as exclusive ticketing agent.

## 2. Definitions

**Gross Ticket Revenue (GTR)** means face value ticket sales plus facility fees collected, excluding GST, refunds, and chargebacks.

**Net Collectible Revenue (NCR)** means GTR less payment processing fees (2.9% + $0.30 per order), fraud reserves released after 30 days, and approved charity allocations.

## 3. Revenue waterfall

NCR is applied in the following order:

1. **Ticketing Platform service fee:** 3.25% of GTR plus AUD $0.95 per ticket issued.  
2. **Venue facility recovery:** 12% of NCR after step 1.  
3. **Promoter marketing recoup:** actual documented paid media up to **AUD $180,000** or 18% of remaining pool, whichever is lower.  
4. **Balance split:** Promoter **62%**, Venue **38%**.

## 4. Settlement mechanics

TicketRiver holds funds in a dedicated trust sub-account. Within **5 business days** after each show:

- TicketRiver releases Venue facility recovery;  
- Promoter receives marketing recoup statement;  
- residual balance distributed per clause 3.4.

Monthly true-up reconciles chargebacks against future settlements.

## 5. Ticketing Platform obligations

- provide API access for capacity holds;  
- implement dynamic pricing rules approved in writing;  
- deliver buyer data subject to Privacy Act consents;  
- maintain PCI DSS compliance.

## 6. Promoter obligations

- artist guarantees and show delivery;  
- marketing spend reporting within **72 hours** of each invoice;  
- indemnify Venue and TicketRiver for title defects in marketing assets.

## 7. Venue obligations

- capacity 8,500 seated;  
- ushers and medics;  
- settlement sign-off within **48 hours** of TicketRiver statement.

## 8. Chargeback risk

If chargebacks exceed 1.2% of GTR for a show, Promoter funds the excess until reconciled from subsequent dates.

## 9. Termination

Material breach: **10 business days** cure period. Insolvency triggers immediate suspension of new ticket on-sales.

## 10. Dispute resolution

Expert determination by qualified entertainment accountant before litigation.
