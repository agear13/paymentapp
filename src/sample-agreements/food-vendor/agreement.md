# FOOD VENDOR CONCESSION AGREEMENT

**Date:** 6 October 2026

**Venue Operator:** **Riverfront Markets Pty Ltd** (ABN 31 445 667 889)  
**Vendor:** **Taco Libre Pty Ltd** (ABN 42 556 778 990)

## 1. Concession

Vendor operates a Mexican street food stall at Riverfront Night Markets each Friday in November 2026.

## 2. Fees

(a) **Site fee:** AUD $850 per night plus GST.  
(b) **Sales participation:** Vendor pays **12% of gross food sales** excluding GST.

## 3. Reporting

Vendor submits nightly sales summary by **10:00 am** the following day through the Venue's vendor portal.

## 4. Payment

Site fee due **48 hours before** each market night. Sales participation remitted within **5 business days** after each night.

## 5. Vendor obligations

- hold Food Business Licence and current council registration;  
- maintain allergen signage;  
- use Venue-approved grease disposal;  
- staff wear high-visibility aprons provided by Vendor.

## 6. Venue obligations

Provide 3m x 3m pitch, power (10 amp), and fresh water access.

## 7. Health and safety

Venue may shut down stall for temperature control breach without refund of site fee.

## 8. Termination

Either party terminates with **14 days' notice** after first market night.
